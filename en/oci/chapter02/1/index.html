2.1 Creating an Oracle Cloud Free Tier Account

To try Oracle Cloud Infrastructure for free, we provide a 30 day trial that’s available within the US 300$ limit. It is also free to use after 30 days for Always Free resources.

  • https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/

  • List of available services in 30-day free trial
  • https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/#free-cloud-trial

Procedure for creating an Oracle Cloud Free Tier account

  1. Free Tier Application
  • Go to https://www.oracle.com/kr/cloud/free/ and click to start for free.

  1. Enter Account Information

  1. After you enter the basic information and click Confirm My Email, the link will be delivered to the email you entered.

  1. Click Click here and you will be directed to the Free Tier Registration link.

  1. Go to the registration link and enter additional account information.
  • Enter your password

  • Cloud Account Name

  • Home Region

Free Tier is a region. No additional modification is possible.

  • Terms of Use

  • Address Information

  • Mobile Phone Number

  • Payment Validation

  • Start your free trial
